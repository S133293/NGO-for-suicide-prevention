Suicide Is Never The Answer

More then 7,00,000 people die by suicide every year; that's one person every 40 seconds.It have a ripple effect that impacts on our family, friends and also our society. While the link between suicide and mental disorders(in particular, depression and alcohol use disorders ) is well established,many suicide happen impulsively in moments of crisis.further risk factors include experience of loss, loneliness, discrimination,a relationship break up, financial problems, chronic pain and illness, violence, abuse and conflict or other humanitarian emergencies
